> *(Two-column layout: Left side with text, right side with an image gallery or video embed from past events like the SOL CRAN FEST or Earthship builds.)*

> At Spaces of Learning, we're redefining education for young men by blending hands-on trades like construction, plumbing, electricity, and biodynamic soil science with entrepreneurial skills and spiritual growth. Inspired by concepts like Outward Bound, trades training, and rites of passage, our programs help participants become "spiritual entrepreneurs" who build their own communities.
> 
> The world needs new spaces for discovery—away from conformity and competition. We're addressing the housing crisis, lack of business education, and barriers for low-income families through:  
> - **Affordable Housing Builds:** Upcoming classes in Spring 2026, like the Earthship project in Yerington, Nevada, where participants learn to construct sustainable, low-cost homes using air-crete, domes, and eco-friendly methods.  
> - **Business 101 & Entrepreneurship Courses:** Peer-to-peer learning in real-world skills, from starting ventures to consulting on land use and dome sales.  
> - **Scholarships for Low-Income Families:** Ensuring every young person can access our mentorship programs, vision quests, and training without financial barriers.  
> 
> Your donations and involvement directly fund these initiatives, creating lasting impact in sustainable living, community building, and personal empowerment.
