> ## About Janean
> 
> As the **Director of Instruction at Meadowbrook Country Club** in Ballwin, Missouri, I bring over two decades of experience to help golfers of all levels elevate their game. My journey includes coaching The Woodlands High School Lady Highlanders to three consecutive Texas State Championships (2001–2003), joining the LPGA in 2004, and earning Class A status in 2008. I’ve honed my craft at prestigious clubs across Texas, including Houston Country Club, Lakeside Country Club, and Kingwood’s Golf Advantage School.

> I’m passionate about creating programs that make golf accessible and enjoyable for everyone. My lessons and clinics focus on skill-building, confidence, and fostering a love for the game, whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned player.
