> ### About Audio Video Dimensions
> 
> **Enhancing Dimensions for Sight & Sound**
> 
> Audio Video Dimensions is a premier technology contractor serving the San Francisco Bay Area. Since 1993, we have specialized in the design and installation of high-quality electronic systems for homes and businesses. Our services include custom home theaters, multi-room audio, Dolby Atmos® setups, lighting control, networking, surveillance, and more. We work closely with you to deliver solutions that fit your budget, are intuitive to use, and perform flawlessly—whether the project is large or small, we aim for perfection every time.
> 
> #### Our Mission
> Audio Video Dimensions is dedicated to providing exceptional audio and video experiences tailored to your space. As a team of certified technicians, we focus on creating user-friendly systems that deliver outstanding performance while respecting your vision and resources.
> 
> #### Who We Are
> Founded and owned by Jeff Alegria since 1993, Audio Video Dimensions is proudly based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Jeff's passion for exceptional sound and video began in his teenage years, sparked by early surround-sound experiments that transformed his viewing experience. This hobby evolved into a lifelong career through extensive training and certifications.
> 
> Jeff is a **THX Certified Professional Home Theater II**—the highest level of THX certification—having completed the rigorous Lucasfilm THX program. He maintains strong ties with Dolby Labs professionals (many formerly with Lucasfilm THX). From 1993 to 2008, he was a member of the Custom Electronics Design and Installation Association (CEDIA), earning certifications in Professional Installer, Advanced Home Theater, Multi-Room Audio and Video, Lighting Techniques, and more.
> 
> Additional training includes specialized programs from leading brands such as Lutron, CasaTunes, NUVO, Niles, Russound, RTI, and Universal Remote Control. As a licensed B General Contractor in California, Jeff combines technical expertise with construction knowledge to ensure safe, durable, and high-quality installations that last for years.

> #### Meet the Team: Jeff Alegria – Founder & Senior Technician
> “Using technology is an art that requires a human touch.”
> 
> Jeff Alegria brings theater-quality video and audio to custom spaces with unmatched passion and precision. His journey started as a teenager when his father introduced him to the Teledapter TE-250, a device that simulated surround sound from a mono TV. Fascinated, Jeff sold his Hot Wheels collection to buy a Quadraflex 777 amplifier, combining it with speakers to create his first immersive setup—dreaming of blacked-out windows, painted walls, and a big screen.
> 
> Years of dedicated education, training, and certification followed, leading to his recognition as a THX Certified Professional Home Theater II. Licensed as a General Contractor by the State of California, Jeff upholds the highest standards of professionalism and personalized service.
> 
> Jeff joined Audio Video Dimensions in the early 1990s as an apprentice under the original founder. He transitioned to full ownership in 1995 and has led every aspect of the business ever since. For Jeff, this work feels like destiny—he finds deep satisfaction in seeing clients fully enjoy their enhanced entertainment spaces.
