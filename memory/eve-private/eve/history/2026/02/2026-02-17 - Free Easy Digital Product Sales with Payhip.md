# Free Easy Digital Product Sales with Payhip

> [!INFO] **User** (2026-02-17 09:27:40)

> [!ABSTRACT] **Grok** (2026-02-17 09:27:49)
> One of the easiest and truly free-to-start solutions (no monthly fees, just transaction fees when you make a sale) for selling digital products like a **PDF book** that you can link to from your own website is **Payhip**.
> 
> ### Why Payhip stands out as easy and free
> - **Free forever plan**: $0/month, no credit card required to start. You only pay a 5% transaction fee per sale (plus standard Stripe/PayPal processing fees ~2.9% + $0.30).
> - **Super simple setup**: Upload your PDF, set a price, add a description/cover image, and you're done in minutes—no coding needed.
> - **Instant delivery**: After payment, the customer gets an automatic download link (email + on-screen). It supports PDF stamping (adds buyer info to the file) to discourage sharing/piracy.
> - **Website integration**: Payhip gives you a simple storefront, but the best part for your use case is the **buy-now buttons** or **direct checkout links**. You can embed a button on your website (they provide copy-paste HTML) or just hyperlink a "Buy Now" text/image to your Payhip product page/checkout. It keeps people on or near your site while handling payment and delivery securely.
> - Other nice beginner features: Coupons/discounts, pay-what-you-want pricing, affiliates, basic email collection, EU VAT handling, and custom domain connection (free).
> 
> Sign up at payhip.com, connect Stripe or PayPal, upload your PDF book, and grab the link/button to add to your site. Many creators use this exact setup for ebooks/PDFs because it's beginner-friendly and low-friction.
> 
> ### Other strong free-ish alternatives
> If Payhip doesn't fit, here are the next easiest options (all allow linking from your site):
> 
> - **Gumroad** — Very popular and simple (upload → get link). Free to start, but fees are higher (~10% + $0.50 per sale in many cases). Great checkout experience, but Payhip edges it out on lower fees and extras like PDF stamping.
> - **SendOwl** or similar add-ons — Good for embedding buy buttons on your site, but often less "free forever" or more limited without paid tiers.
> 
> Avoid platforms like Sellfy (now starts at ~$22/month after trial, no true free plan) if you want zero upfront/monthly costs.
> 
> Start with Payhip—it's one of the most recommended right now for exactly this scenario (PDF/digital book sales linked from a personal website). If your sales grow, you can upgrade to their paid plans later to drop the platform fee to 2% or 0%. Good luck with your PDF book!
